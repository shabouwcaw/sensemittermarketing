FTUE Emotional Benchmarking
  • Weak Day-1 retention
 Emotion analysis of the early game experience, uncovering the precise moments of friction or delight. Pinpoint friction, double-down on what's working and lift Day-1 retention.
Emo-Playtesting
  • High attrition at key gameplay moments or feature introduction
 Tracking emotional feedback of live sessions for any portion of the game. Isolate the split-second moments where the game works or doesn't, without interrupting the flow or relying on the user to remember the problem later.
Emo-UX Testing
  • Usability issues distorting how players judge the game — and your prioritization of fixes
 Combines traditional UX testing with emotion tracking to reveal not just what's broken, but how it feels. Reveals both the UX issues and their emotional costs. Knowing the intensity of frustration associated with each issue enables a studio to effectively prioritize which fixes will yield the greatest impact on player enjoyment.
Video Creative Testing
  • Low ad-to-store conversion
 Analyzing the emotional response to video assets to evaluate user appeal and engagement, providing frame-by-frame, subconscious insights. Discover which creative elements drive or diminish desire and anticipation.
App/Storefront Asset Optimization (ASO)
  • Poor visitors-to-player acquisition
 Quantifying the subconscious impact of icons, screenshots, and descriptions using real or mock store pages. Find the components within assets that resonate and drive higher install intent.
