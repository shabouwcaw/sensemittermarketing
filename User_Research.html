Biometric data has value.
We make it actionable.

Decades of research shows that physiological data can reveal moments missed by surveys and observation. Until now, applying these methods in games meant lab setups, specialist hardware, and slow analysis. Emhance AI makes these signals usable in real playtests, without labs or complex setups.

Lab-grade signals, natural play

Players test at home on their own devices, reducing observation effects and keeping context intact.

Camera-based access

Capture facial and attention signals without electrodes, head-mounted rigs, or calibration-heavy setups.

From signals to research outputs

Our neural net highlights peaks, drop-offs, and confusion zones, linked to game events and UI states.

Method-aware framing

Signals are treated as diagnostic inputs, not literal emotional truth, with transparency about what is inferred.

Built for triangulation

The Emhance AI methodology combines cutting edge emotion recognition and neural nets with traditional observation, interviews and surverys, providing a 360-degree view into player behavior.

Repeatable research

Research use cases

Where it fits in your workflow

Locate experience breaks, prioritise probes, and align stakeholders on what evidence supports a change.

Usability diagnostics

Pinpoint the exact seconds where comprehension breaks, attention drops, or control confidence collapses.

Hypothesis refinement

Use peaks and drop-offs to refine what to ask next and what variants to test, before running broader studies.

Experience mapping

Build a timeline of attention and activation across tasks, levels, or journeys, tied to events and UI states.

Stakeholder alignment
