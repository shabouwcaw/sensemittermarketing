Mission

Make emotion the universal signal
behind great digital experiences.

Teams already have plenty of data. What they often lack is the “why” behind player decisions. We measure emotion moment-by-moment, then translate it into actions that fit real development timelines.

Passion for great games

From creatives, to research to design we bring a wealth of expertise to make gaming great. We don't just measure outcomes; we bleed gaming.

Decisions you can ship

Our story

Why Emhance AI exists

Traditional research often tells teams what players did, but it struggles to explain why those decisions happened in the moment. Emotion and attention signals surface the hidden moments: where excitement builds, where frustration appears, and where attention drops — so teams can fix the right problems first.
