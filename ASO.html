Applications

What to test in ASO?

Understand which store elements move players from curious to convinced.

Icon & Title

Measure first-glance impact. See which combinations create fast recognition and higher conversion potential in crowded categories.

Screenshot Order

Analyse attention distribution per frame. Optimise carousel order to keep interest high instead of dropping off after screenshot two.

Video Preview

Find the exact second where viewers stop watching. Modify trailers to front-load the beats that create excitement and clarity.

Taglines & Copy

Compare emotional lift between value propositions. See which lines resonate for midcore, casual, or genre-specific audiences.

Ratings & Social Proof

Test how different ratings, review snippets, and badges shift trust and conversion at a glance.

Competitor Pages

Run tests on top-performing competitors. Deconstruct their “secret sauce” and borrow the emotional beats that work.

Workflow

How ASO testing works

1

Upload store variants

Add icons, screenshots, videos, and copy variants to the dashboard. Define the KPIs you care about most.

2

Recruit players

Use your own audience or our gamer panels by GEO and genre. Participants view your mocked-up store pages on desktop or mobile.

3

Capture emotion & gaze

We record their session with camera and screen, capturing hundreds of emotion and attention signals every second.

4

AI decoding

Neural networks map gaze paths, emotional peaks, confusion zones, and stated intent to each component.

5

Actionable report

Get heatmaps, attention curves, and ranked variants with clear “keep / cut / iterate” guidance for each concept.

6

Launch with confidence

Take only the strongest variants into live A/B tests. Reduce test cycles, spend less to find winners, and scale what works.
